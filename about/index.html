About Me

  2019-02-28  Comments Word Count: 243words Read Count: 2minutes

I am a freelance IT professional, and besides visualization I focus on Golang development and infrastructure automation (DevOps?).

In past I worked in Google in Dublin, in CERN in Geneva, I did contract work for Basho, the makers of Riak, and Rovio, the company behind the Angry Birds. I wrote Ansible modules for the Packet host (acquired by Equinix in 2020 and rebranded as Equinix Metal). I am maintaining Equinix Metal Terraform Provider and a couple of other intrastructure-related Golang-based tools. I am Ansible and Terraform contributor.

I have CS bachelor’s degree from Brno University of Technology (2006) and CS master’s degree from University of Eastern Finland (2011).

I speak Czech (native), English (OK), Finnish (~B2 level), and French (~pas tres mal).

Back in the day I did some low-profile talks in Helsinki and online:

  • Using Ansible on OpenStack at OpenStackFin Helsinki meetup 23.10.2014
  • Demo: Using Docker with Ansible at Ansible Helsinki meetup 29.10.2014
  • Bootstrapping Drupal 8 with Docker at Docker meetup in Helsinki 29.9.2015
  • Using Ansible on the Packet Host, online demo 2.6.2017
  • Practical Intro to Go, presentation in Coding Sauba in TribeTampere, 28.3.2022
  • How Bitcoin Works, presentation in Coding Sauna in TribeTampere, 11.4.2022

In 2019 I won a challenge on “Averaging GPS segments” from the Machine Learning Deparment at the University of Eastern Finland. This is the Jupyter notebook with my solution.
